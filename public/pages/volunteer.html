Get Involved

Camp Salvation is 100% Volunteered camp, we focus on helping people out. Our staff is made up of all sorts of people with skills that come from God. We would love to have you join our staff and help camp salvation
If God is calling you to volunteer, please fill out and application. We are blessed to have you!

Camp Nurse

This is Child Care licensing requirement by the State of Colorado. We need a nurse every week we run camp. They are responsible to be a role model to campers and share the Gospel as opportunity arises.

Head Cook

TThis position is filled by appointment from the Director. This person is responsible for planning menus and ordering food. They are the direct supervisor of the other cooks and helpers when at Camp. They are responsible to be a role model to campers and share the Gospel as opportunity arises

Cooks

They help the Head Cook prepare the meals and keep the kitchen running smooth. Two to three are needed, in addition to the Lead Cook, each week. They are responsible to be a role model to campers and share the Gospel as opportunity arises.

Counselors

These are adult sponsors who stay in the cabins with the campers and are required to be 18 or older. We need 4 female and 4 male counselors each week at the bare minimum but prefer having two per cabin, meaning 8 of each. We will cap the number of counselors at 16 each week. These should be mature, committed Christians who love God, and would give good Biblical counsel to the campers. They are responsible to be a role model to campers and share the Gospel as opportunity arises.

Helpers

TWe need eight to twelve helpers each week. There is a cap on the number of helpers accepted each week. They must be 16 or older. They report to the Lead Cook and assignments can include: keeping the facilities clean, any assigned kitchen prep, and are responsible to be a role model to campers and share the Gospel as opportunity arises.
