Campers will not be considered fully registered until all paperwork has been received by Camp Salvation, no later than two weeks prior to arrival.

Campers may only register for one week.

Cancellations made more than two weeks before the date registered will be fully refunded. Any cancellation after that time will only be refunded for medical reasons and must be accompanied by a letter from the attending physician.

Camp Salvation reserves the right to dismiss any camper whom the staff believes presents a safety concern, medial risk, is disruptive, or otherwise conducts themselves in a manner detrimental to the camp community, no refund will be issued in the case of dismissals.

Rules for acceptance are the same for everyone without regard to race, color, nationality, gender, religion, or disability. Camp Salvation is not a treatment center and all our staff are volunteers. For the safety and well-being of our campers we do reserve the right to refuse admission to our programs, in appropriate cases; per the General Rules for Child Care Facilities 7.701.14 and 7.702.61c.
