Get Involved

Camp Salvation is staffed 100% by volunteers. From the Directors preparing each week for months before, to the cooks creating each meal, to the helpers cleaning every day, each person is there to give of their time for the glory of God. We would love to have you join our staff.

Because we are licensed as a child care provider by the Colorado Department of Human Services, we do require three references and a background check for all of our staff. Contact us for more information.

If God is calling you to volunteer, please complete the correct application. We would love to serve alongside you!
