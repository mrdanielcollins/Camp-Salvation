About Camp

Our Activites

Boating : Camp Salvation sits just above beautiful North Lake, giving us a unique opportunity to spend time out in a canoe or paddle boat.

Sand Volleyball : A favorite activity of campers, you can always join a pick-up game!

Game Room : Complete with ping-pong, air-hockey, and carpetball! Get to know a new friend with some friendly competition.

Hiking : Campers and staff take the day to explore one of the local trails and enjoy the beautiful world that God has created for us.

Basketball : We have an indoor half-court and another hoop outside, challenge some friends to a game or shoot-out.

Prayer walk : Signs with a message and verses will direct you down the path as you slow down and spend some time alone with God

Obstacle course : Work as a team to complete challenges and grow closer; all while enjoying the beautiful forest around the camp

Rec time : Time to compete! Throughout the day, teams compete in different activities in the battle to be the champions.

Praise and worship : We start each chapel service worshiping together to prepare our heart’s to hear God’s word.

Chapel : Teaching from God’s word to introduce campers to Christ, and encourage those who already have a relationship with Him, how to continue living for Him.
