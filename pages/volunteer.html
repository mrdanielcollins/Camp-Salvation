Get Involved

Camp Salvation is staffed 100% by volunteers. From the Directors preparing each week for months before, to the cooks creating each meal, to the helpers cleaning every day, each person is there to give of their time for the glory of God. We would love to have you join our staff.

Because we are licensed as a child care provider by the Colorado Department of Human Services, we do require three references and a background check for all of our staff. Contact us for more information.

If God is calling you to volunteer, please contact us. Staff Applications coming soon. Please check back April 1st. Thank you!

Helper

The foundation of camp! Serving by cleaning, assisting in the kitchen, and anywhere else needed; they help make the camp environment safe, clean, comfortable, and friendly for all.

Counselor

Assigned to a specific cabin, these staff are the direct supervision and leader of campers in their cabin. Responsible for campersâ€™ whereabouts and safety, must be 18 years or older to serve in this position.

Nurse

As the lead medical professional of the week, these staff create a safe environment and address medical issues as needed. As a licensed child care provider, there are requirements that must be met for medical staff at camp, please contact for more information.

Cooks

Meal time is the heart of relationship building at camp. Comfortable food, friendly faces, gracious servers, and sweet conversations all help to build an environment where our campers and our staff feel loved and accepted.

Praise and Worship Team

If you have a passion or gift for music and would like to be a part of the worship team while at camp, we would love to have you involved. This is an additional role, please let your director know if you are interested in helping here as well.

Recreation Director

Helping campers have fun together and make memories, the rec director plans games to help campers break down barriers and build relationships with other campers as well as with staff who could mentor them. This position will require director approval to apply.

Maintenance

Camp activities create some wear and tear on camp facilities. We need help maintaining a safe environment for all.
