Getting Here

Don't Get Lost On Your Way To Camp!



Camp Salvation is located in one of the most beautiful places in Colorado, the heart of the Rockies. Surrounded by God’s brilliant, creative nature in the beauty He has created for us to enjoy.

From Interstate 25 North of Walsenburg- Take the Highway 160 exit to LaVeta, about 12 miles from Walsenburg take the Highway 12 turn, after about 30 miles once you go around North Lake, look for the Camp Salvation sign on the Left side of the road.

From Interstate 25 South of Trinidad – take exit 14a and follow Highway 12 past the High School and out of town, Camp is about 40 miles from Trinidad. After passing Monument Lake, look for the Camp Salvation sign on the Right side of the road.
